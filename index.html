About Me

In the bleak and desolate future of 2017, Humans must fight to survive. Beneath the dark cloud of fallout left by the detonation of the psychic nuclear warhead that brought an abrupt end to Mega-World War 3, one man still codes. One man still indents his work, whatever the cost.

Get ready for a non-stop thrill ride through the most exciting HTML ever to render on to the big screen! This summer, Andrew Galipeau stars in "LazerCode: Stay DRY or Die Trying" - Coming to a theater near you!
